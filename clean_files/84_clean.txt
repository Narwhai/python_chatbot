biking is a song by american singer-songwriter frank ocean, released as a single on the third episode of blonded radio.
it features guest vocals from american rappers jay-z and tyler, the creator.
it was re-released as a solo version, after the sixth episode of blonded radio.
it was featured in season 2 of the hbo television series insecure.
biking followed the release of two other frank ocean songs: slide and chanel.
the song was produced by frank dukes, jarami, and caleb laven.
