"Biking" is a song by American singer-songwriter Frank Ocean, released as a single on the third episode of Blonded Radio. It features guest vocals from American rappers Jay-Z and Tyler, The Creator.[1] It was re-released as a solo version, after the sixth episode of Blonded Radio. It was featured in Season 2 of the HBO television series Insecure.
"Biking" followed the release of two other Frank Ocean songs: "Slide" and "Chanel". The song was produced by Frank Dukes, Jarami, and Caleb Laven.
